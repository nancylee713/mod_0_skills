Class: School

Attributes
- schoolName (string)
- numberOfStudents (integer)
- studentTeacherRatio (string)
- coEd (Boolean)
- startDate (datetime)
- tuition (float)
- facultyCatalogue (array)

Methods
- addNewStudent (numberOfStudents + 1)
- changeStartDate (startDate +/- x days)
- increaseTuition (tuition + tuition * x%)
- isCoEd (check the value of coEd attribute)
- addFaculty (add new teachers to facultyCatalogue attribute)
